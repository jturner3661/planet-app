Click the buttons below to find out about terrestrial or Gas Giant planets!
Gas Giants: The outer four planets, which are made entirely of gas.

Jupiter: The fifth planet from the Sun and the largest in the Solar System.

Saturn: The sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest in the Solar System.

Uranus:  The seventh planet from the Sun, with the third largest radius in the solar system.

Neptune: The eighth planet from the Sun and the farthest known solar planet. In the Solar System, it is the fourth-largest planet by diameter.
Terrestrial Planets: The inner 4 planets made of rock.

Mercury: The closest planet to the sun and the smallest planet in the solar system

Venus: It is hotter than mercury despite being further away, and it spins clockwise on its axis. its also known as earths twin/sister.

Earth: The planet we live on, and only known object in space to have life.

Mars: Mars is known as the red planet and has 2 moons.
